# Heckbound Idol Quest 

A mischievous red Imp embarks on a chaotic journey from Hell to Earth and finally Heaven, all to get an autograph from an angel idol. Navigate whimsical worlds, dodge cute monsters, and solve playful puzzles in this humorous pixel platformer adventure.

---

## Planned Story Structure

### **Act I — Hell (9 Levels)**

1. Limbo – Waiting Room
2. Lust – Lovebug Lounge
3. Gluttony – Snackpit Station
4. Greed – Vault of Vultures
5. Wrath – Tantrum Tunnels
6. Heresy – Blasphemy Botanical Garden
7. Violence – Scorchshaft
8. Fraud – Frozen Fraud Fields
9. Treachery – Portal Room B-12

### **Act II — Earth (4 Levels)**

10. Alley of Confusion
11. Metro Mayhem
12. Rooftop Riot
13. Sky Broadcast Tower

### **Act III — Heaven (3 Levels + Final Boss)**

14. Cloudtop Gardens
15. Choir Corridor
16. Halo Heights: Fan Club Entrance
17. Star Halo Amphitheater – Final Boss / Idol Encounter

## Features (Planned)

* Comedic pixel platformer with tight movement
* Unique level gimmicks and puzzles per stage
* Cute, whimsical monsters and enemies
* Player forms: normal Imp, fire Imp, ghost Imp, etc.
* Boss encounters with humorous mechanics
